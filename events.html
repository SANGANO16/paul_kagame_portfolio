Community

Umuganda - National Community Work

8:00 AM - 11:00 AM Nationwide All Citizens

Umuganda is Rwanda's monthly national community work program held on the last Saturday of every month. Citizens come together to work on community projects such as building roads, cleaning public spaces, planting trees, and helping vulnerable community members. It is a powerful expression of Rwandan unity and collective responsibility.

Kwibuka - Genocide Commemoration

100 Days Nationwide & Diaspora All Rwandans

The annual commemoration period begins on April 7th and lasts for 100 days, marking the duration of the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi. The period includes memorial services, educational programs, and community dialogues about unity and reconciliation.
