Welcome

Paul Kagame is the President of the Republic of Rwanda, a position he has held since 2000. Known for his transformative leadership, he has guided Rwanda through a period of remarkable recovery and growth following the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi.

His administration is defined by a commitment to peace, reconciliation, and economic self-reliance. Under his guidance, Rwanda has become a global model for development, women's empowerment, and technological innovation.
