Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How can I invite President Kagame to an event?

Event invitations should be sent at least 3 months in advance through the official contact form or by email to events@paulkagame.rw. Please include details about the event, date, location, and expected audience.

How can I request an interview with the President?

Media interview requests should be directed to press@paulkagame.rw with details about your media outlet, the purpose of the interview, and proposed dates. Our press team will review and respond to your request.

Where can I find official speeches and documents?

All official speeches, policy documents, and publications are available in the Resources section of this website. You can download PDF versions of most documents.

How can I stay updated on news and events?

You can stay updated by visiting our News section, subscribing to our newsletter on the Events page, or following our official social media accounts.
