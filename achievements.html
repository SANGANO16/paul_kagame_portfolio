Major Achievements

A Legacy of Transformation and Progress

Post-Genocide Stability

Successfully guided Rwanda from the devastation of the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi towards a path of national reconciliation, unity, and peace. This has laid the foundation for all other achievements. The Gacaca courts processed over 1.2 million cases, promoting justice and healing.

Healthcare Revolution

Implemented the Mutuelle de Santé, a community-based health insurance scheme that covers over 90% of the population. Significant reductions in child and maternal mortality rates have been achieved. Life expectancy has increased from 49 to 69 years.

ICT & Innovation

Transformed Rwanda into a leading ICT hub in Africa. The nationwide fiber-optic network and the Smart Rwanda Master Plan have accelerated digital inclusion and innovation across the country. Kigali is now known as the "Singapore of Africa."

Women Empowerment

Rwanda holds the world record for the highest percentage of women in parliament, currently exceeding 60%. This commitment to gender equality has been a central pillar of national development, with women leading in business, politics, and education.

Environmental Protection

Championed environmental sustainability, including the nationwide ban on plastic bags and the annual Umuganda (community work) that includes massive tree-planting efforts. Rwanda is committed to becoming carbon neutral by 2050.

Urban Development

Transformed Kigali into one of the cleanest and safest cities in Africa. Major infrastructure projects like the Kigali Convention Centre have made the city a hub for international conferences and tourism, earning it the nickname "Africa's Cleanest City."

Rwanda: Before & After

Before 1994

  • Extreme poverty and economic instability
  • High ethnic tensions and divisionism
  • Minimal infrastructure and healthcare access
  • Low global presence and investment
  • Limited educational opportunities
  • Political instability and conflict

Today

  • Fastest-growing economy in the region
  • National unity and reconciliation
  • Universal healthcare and high literacy rates
  • Top destination for MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions)
  • World-class education system with digital learning
  • Stable democracy with strong institutions
